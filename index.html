How it was calculated

This tool applies the VA Combined Ratings Table formula (38 CFR §4.25) and the bilateral factor (38 CFR §4.26) as a general-purpose estimate. It does not account for every regulatory nuance (e.g. special monthly compensation, TDIU, or specific paired-muscle-group rules) and is not an official VA determination.
